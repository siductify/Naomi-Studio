Two Dorms, One Vision.

Published: APRIL 2026 Read Time: 4 MIN
"We didn't start with a business plan. We started with a frustration that the fastest hardware in the world was being used to load websites that felt like they were stuck in 2005."

Naomi Studios began with a simple observation: many institutions were being sold complex backend systems they didn’t actually need. For businesses like cafés, gyms, and salons, this added unnecessary costs, increased maintenance, and introduced complications that slowed everything down instead of improving it.

We saw a clear problem—websites were becoming heavier, more expensive, and harder to manage, even when a streamlined frontend was all that was required. That’s where we stepped in.

The "Frontend-Only" Epiphany

Naomi Studios is a newborn, student-founded company built on the idea that simplicity is powerful. We focus on creating clean, efficient, frontend-first websites that remove unnecessary layers and deliver faster, smoother experiences for both businesses and their customers.
